Hello!My name's Evelyn Youssef and I am currently pursuing a Bachelors in Fine Arts majoring in graphic design at the University of Massachusetts Dartmouth. I am an artist who loves to create work that cultivates and highlights mixed media into my pieces. My favorite mediums to work with include acrylic paint, permanent markers, and archival ink pens. I began my education with design in 2014 when I attended Blackstone Valley Technical High School being in the vocation Multimedia Communications. This program gave me opportunities to learn basic design skills in advertisement and design, photography and film, as well as web development. When graduating in 2018, I was certified in Adobe Photoshop CC 2015 and Osha 10 Hour Training General Industry. While studying at Umass Dartmouth, I have effectively learned the creative design process - which allows me to create appropriate and efficient projects accordingly. Check Out My Resume
